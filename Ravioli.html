Ravioli: A dish for Me, just me.... I am not sharing.

I LOVE RAVIOLI! IT IS THE FREAKING BEST!

For the Dough:

  • Ipo 00 Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil

For the Filling:

  • Whole Milk Ricotta
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheeseli>
  • MOAR EGG!

Step #1: Make Pasta Dough

  1. There are several ways to make fresh pasta dough. The food processor method is the fastest. To do so: add the flour, eggs and olive oil to a food processor fitted with the metal blade. Process to form dough, about 15-20 seconds. Remove to your work surface and cover with an upturned bowl for 5 minutes- it will be much easier to knead in the next step.

  2. Knead dough for 1-2 minutes to form a ball (donâ€™t worry about technique here, the food processor does most of the kneading for you) cover again, and let rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour to relax the gluten. Use this time to make the ravioli filling.

Step #2: Make Ravioli Filling

  1. While the pasta dough is resting: combine the drained ricotta, mascarpone, chopped basil leaves, Parmesan cheese and egg. Season with salt and pepper; mix well. Give it a taste (it should be highly seasoned). Cover and chill until ready to use. This step can be done 1-2 days in advance.
